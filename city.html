Designs

Monochromatic Palette

A monochromatic color palette is a color scheme that is based on variations of a single color. It includes different shades, tints, and tones of that color, creating a harmonious and unified look.

Analogous Palette

An analogous color palette is a harmonious color scheme that consists of colors that are adjacent to each other on the color wheel. These colors share a similar undertone or hue, which creates a sense of unity and cohesion in a design.

Complementary Palette

A complementary color palette is a color scheme that consists of two colors that are opposite to each other on the color wheel. These colors provide the highest level of contrast and harmony when used together.

Triadic Palette

A triadic color palette is a color scheme that consists of three colors that are evenly spaced around the color wheel, forming an equilateral triangle. This results in a balanced and dynamic combination of colors.
